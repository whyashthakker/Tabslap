## How to Install Ask AI Chrome Extension

### 4.1. System Requirements

To install the Ask AI Chrome Extension, ensure that your system meets the following requirements:
- Google Chrome browser (version 92 or above)
- Active internet connection
- Sufficient storage space

### 4.2. Step-by-Step Installation Guide

Follow these simple steps to install the Ask AI Chrome Extension:
1. Open the Google Chrome browser on your device.
2. Visit the official Ask AI website or the Chrome Web Store.
3. Locate the Ask AI Chrome Extension and click on the "Add to Chrome" button.
4. A pop-up window will appear, requesting permission to add the extension. Click on "Add extension" to proceed.
5. The extension will be downloaded and installed automatically. Once the installation is complete, the Ask AI icon will appear in your Chrome browser's toolbar.

### 4.3. Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter any issues during the installation process or while using the Ask AI Chrome Extension, consider the following troubleshooting tips:
- Ensure that your Chrome browser is up to date.
- Check your internet connection and make sure it is stable.
- Clear your Chrome browser cache and restart the browser.
- Disable any conflicting extensions or plugins that may interfere with the Ask AI Chrome Extension.
- If the issue persists, contact the Ask AI support team for further assistance.
